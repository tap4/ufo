The Truth is Out There: An Analysis of UFO Data

The National UFO Reporting Center Online Database tracks UFO Reports by Event Date, Report Date, U.S. State of occurence, and Shape of UFO. They provide the following disclaimer:

The National UFO Reporting Center makes no claims as to the validity of the information in any of these reports. Obvious hoaxes have been omitted, however most reports have been posted exactly as received in the author's own words.
A simplistic analysis of the data (shown on the right) presents an obvious and alarming trend over the last 20 years. The frequency of reported UFO sightings has grown exponentially.
